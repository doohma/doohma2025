Privacy Policy

Doohma offers a range of digital solutions, including digital out-of-home hyper-local advertisement services, data-driven tech training through Datamug.io, and social media migration via GoPost. This privacy notice states our commitment towards data protection by being transparent about the data we collect about you, how it is used, and with whom it is shared. It aims to inform you about how we collect, use, disclose, and store information about you when you:

  • Interact or use our website, including submitting personal data and advertisement content.
  • Register and/or attend any of our events.
  • If you use our applications: Datamug.io for data training, GoPost for social media content migration, or engage with our digital advertising services via Doohma.

1. Why We Collect Your Information

We collect personal data from you for the following:

  • 1. At the time of registration – When you register to create a user account with us, the following information is collected: (1) Name, (2) Address (including post code), (3) Contact phone number, and (4) credit card information.
  • 2. Your Profile – The choices selected by you to appear on your profile such as contact information, employment history, preferences, picture, news feeds, etc.
  • 3. Posting and uploading information – If you sync your social media (e.g. Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.) professional profile with our Services, we will collect your connections information, contact details, and group information to keep growing your network by suggesting connections for you and others.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any of the information we collect from you may be used to:

  • 1. Suggest additional connections that match your profile and selected preferences.
  • 2. Personalize our services.
  • 3. Send promotional communications, targeting advertising (including relevant event offers).
  • 4. Improve and develop our products and services.

3. Accuracy of Information

The personal information shared on Doohma’s application shall be kept up-to-date by contacting you. If there are any changes in personal circumstances, you should update your profile or contact us directly (refer to below section 11.0).

4. Distribution of Information

We do not send personal information to third parties for their sole promotional purposes or as otherwise outlined in this Privacy Notice.

To support business activities, the transfer of personal information collected through our website and application to third parties located outside Doohma’s country of business may be required. To transfer data, the following permissible data transfer mechanisms will be applied:

  • 1. Data Transfer Agreement with third parties
  • 2. Notice to and/or approval from a country’s local data protection authority

5. Your Privacy Rights

For personal information provided to us, you have control of this information, which includes the right to access, modify, erase, restrict, transmit, or object to certain uses of personal information. You can control your personal data that Doohma has obtained, and exercise your data protection rights, by contacting Doohma Support (admin@Doohma.com).

6. Consent

Agreeing to this privacy notice will be treated as consent to process your personal information for the above-defined purposes. Your consent to personal data collection and processing may be revoked by notifying us by raising your request to Doohma’s DPO (refer to below section 10).

For users below the age of 16, consent should be provided by the holder of parental responsibility of the child.

7. Website Tracking

We use cookies or similar technologies to identify your browser or device. Mostly, first-party cookies are used to store information on your device to remember information about you, such as login information.

Cookie information will also be used in our advertising and marketing efforts, as well as to understand your browsing of the Website (for example, which page you visit or how long you stay on each page).

8. Data Security

Doohma has implemented reasonable technical and organizational measures to safeguard personal information and instruct third parties processing personal information on behalf of Doohma to ensure consistent and sufficient controls are applied to protect personal information.

9. Retention and Disposal

We will retain personal information collected through our website and application as per the defined retention schedule, which is in alignment with local laws and regulations.
